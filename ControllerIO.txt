Controller IO Notes

	Analog input for right speed (Pot)
	Analog input for arm position (Pot)
	digital input for control state (Toggle Switch)
	2 digital inputs for gripper movement (2 Buttons)
Outputs
	RX and TX for commmunications
	LED for communication 
	LED for power
